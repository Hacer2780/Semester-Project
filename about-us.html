Welcome to the Community Science Museum!

At the Community Science Museum, we believe in the power of science to inspire, educate, and bring people together. Our mission is to make science accessible to everyone, fostering curiosity and a lifelong love for discovery.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, the Community Science Museum was established to provide a space where people of all ages could explore the wonders of science. From its humble beginnings with just a few exhibits, the museum has grown to become a hub for learning and innovation, with over 1,000 interactive exhibits spanning various scientific disciplines.

Our museum celebrates the achievements of science throughout history while looking forward to the discoveries of tomorrow. From the ancient wonders of astronomy to cutting-edge robotics and artificial intelligence, we aim to ignite a passion for learning and exploration in every visitor.

What We Offer

  • Explore our hands-on displays in fields such as physics, biology, robotics, and ecology.
  • Workshops & Events: From holiday science clubs for kids to professional training for teachers, we host a wide range of activities.
  • Research Opportunities: Partner with universities and researchers to use our resources for groundbreaking studies.
